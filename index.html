Our mission

Online dating taught a generation to swipe fast and feel less. We are building the opposite. Biya is a social network where positivity is the product, where being kind is rewarded, and where meeting someone feels hopeful again.

How it works

Positivity, built into the product.

Not a feature we bolt on later. The way Biya is designed from the first tap.

Kindness is the score

People rise by being thoughtful, not by gaming an algorithm. Respect earns reach.

Intentions up front

No mind reading. People share what they are looking for, so every hello starts honest.

Safe by design

Calm by default. Strong protections, gentle pacing, and a community that self-corrects.

3x
more second dates in early testing*
Why now

People are tired of feeling worse after they log on.

Dating has become a numbers game that leaves everyone a little colder. Biya bets on the opposite instinct: that given a kinder room, people show up as their better selves, and stay.

  • A community built on respect, not performance
  • Signals that reward kindness and follow-through
  • A brand people are proud to be seen using
